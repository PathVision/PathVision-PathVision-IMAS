Road Accidents Don't Happen Suddenly

Most serious accidents are the result of ignored warning signs that build up over time.

Driver fatigue and distraction

Reduced alertness and attention silently increase the risk of critical driving errors.

Human reaction time is limited

In sudden or complex situations, human responses are often too slow to prevent impact.

Safety systems react too late

Most existing systems respond only after danger becomes unavoidable.

Road awareness is fragmented

Critical insights are scattered across disconnected tools instead of a single intelligent system.
